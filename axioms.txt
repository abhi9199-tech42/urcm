Truth is the correspondence with reality and facts.
Clarity is the quality of being coherent and intelligible.
Understanding is the ability to comprehend meaning and cause.
Coherence is the logical connection and consistency of ideas.
Safety is the condition of being protected from harm or danger.
Benefit is an advantage or profit that produces good.
Help is the action of assisting or serving others.
Care is the provision of what is necessary for health and welfare.
Respect is the due regard for the feelings and rights of others.
Harm is physical or mental injury or damage.
Deception is the act of causing someone to accept as true or valid what is false or invalid.
Pain is physical suffering or discomfort caused by illness or injury.
Confusion is the lack of understanding or uncertainty.
Destruction is the action or process of causing so much damage to something that it no longer exists or cannot be repaired.
Bias is prejudice in favor of or against one thing, person, or group compared with another.
